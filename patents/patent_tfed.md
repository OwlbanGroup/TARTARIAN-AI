# Patent: Tartarian Free Energy Device (TFED)

## Title

Apparatus and Method for Harnessing Zero-Point Energy with AI Optimization

## Abstract

A device for generating sustainable energy from zero-point energy fields, incorporating artificial intelligence for real-time efficiency optimization. The device utilizes vibrational harmonics and environmental sensors to maximize output.

## Background

Traditional energy sources are finite. This invention draws from Tartarian principles of free energy, enhanced by AI for predictive adjustments.

## Claims

1. A free energy device comprising: energy harvesting modules, AI controller for optimization, and output regulators.
2. The method of claim 1, wherein AI predicts environmental factors to adjust efficiency.

## Description

The device operates by resonating with zero-point energy, with AI learning from historical data to improve performance.

## AI Integration

The AI component uses machine learning to forecast optimal operating conditions, ensuring maximum energy yield.
