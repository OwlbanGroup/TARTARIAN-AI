# Patent: Tartarian Anti-Gravity Craft (TAGC)

## Title
Anti-Gravity Propulsion System with AI Flight Control

## Abstract
A craft utilizing anti-gravity fields for propulsion, with AI for stability and navigation.

## Claims
1. An anti-gravity craft with field generators and AI controller.
2. Method for AI-assisted flight adjustments.

## Description
The craft counters gravitational forces using advanced fields, with AI optimizing trajectory.
