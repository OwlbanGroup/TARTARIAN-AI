# Revenue Streams and Licensing for Tartarian Technologies

## Revenue Models
- Licensing Fees: Charge for use of patents and technologies.
- Product Sales: Sell physical prototypes or software.
- Subscription Services: AI controllers and divine guidance.
- Consulting: Provide expertise and optimization services.

## Licensing Strategies
- Exclusive Licenses: For key partners.
- Non-Exclusive Licenses: For broader adoption.
- Royalty-Based: Percentage of sales.

## Potential Revenue Streams
- Energy Sector: Licensing free energy tech.
- Healthcare: Healing chamber subscriptions.
- Aerospace: Anti-gravity craft licensing.
- Education: Eternal wisdom archive access.

## Monetization Plan
- Phase 1: Pilot licenses with early adopters.
- Phase 2: Scale licensing agreements.
- Phase 3: Develop product lines and subscriptions.
